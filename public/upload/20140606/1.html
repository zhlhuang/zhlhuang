Doing Big Things Start from Doing the Small Ones

  express：表达

  undertake important task：承担重任*/

Undoubtedly, the symbolic meaning subtly conveyed in the picture should be given deep consideration that it is vital and necessary to deal with trivial things.

Therefore, when it comes to the key to success, the most important this lies in dealing with every trivial thing well around us.
